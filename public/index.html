Welcome to Four-In-A-Row!

Rules:

  1. This is a two person game, the game will start when the second person loads their screen
  2. The first person who loads their screen is player one, their color is red
  3. The second person who loads their screen is player two, their color is blue
  4. The object of the game is to get four in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally alternating turns with your opponent
  5. The logic is not yet in place to determine a winner so pay attention to your opponents moves!
  6. Good luck, be honest, and have fun!
